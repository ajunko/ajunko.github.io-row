Rowing: A water sport.

What is it?

Rowing is a hidden gem in the list of sports that athletes around the world partake in year-round. The sport is integrated into the Olympics in which 80 nations from around the world come together to compete on the water. The actual "action" of rowing involves crews of athletes propelling themselves with the use of oars as an extension of their body.

Who can participate?

Everyone who is willing can participate! Rowing does not apply to a strict group of people. Those that compete are made up of verying physical statures, ages, genders, or overall abilities. The sport of rowing is enticing for novices that are both young and old!
